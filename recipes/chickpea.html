Vegan Chickpea Curry

This quick and easy curry using chickpeas and sweet potatoes is vegan and gluten free.You may serve with basmati rice or naan bread for a complete meal. This recipe makes 6 servings. The prep time is 10 minutes. The cook time is 20 minutes. For a total time of 30 minutes. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • one onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 15 ounce can of chickpeas drained
  • 1 14.5 ounce can of crushed tomatos
  • 1 14 ounce can of coconut milk
  • 1 sweet potato cubed
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground tumeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili flakes
  • 1 cup of baby spinach

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Cook onion, garlic, and ginger in hot oil until softened. About 5 minutes.
  3. Add chickpeas, tomatos, coconut milk, and sweet potato.
  4. Bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until tender, about 15 minutes.
  5. Season with garam masala, cumin, tumeric, salt, and chili flakes. Add spinach right before serving.
